How should you take this into account when forming a team?

When we set up a team, we need to make the best of everyone's characteristics. For example, people who have strong leadership and family support are leaders. At the same time, there are some people whose communication ability is not particularly good, and their understanding ability is not particularly good, but they also have a very strong ability to focus. When they are asked to serve as the R & D department or research some profound problems, their role will be infinitely enlarged. So at this time, we need some partners with strong communication ability and empathy ability to communicate between groups. At the same time, we also need some team members with strong ability of discovery, innovation and self-examination, who can help us understand ourselves and find our own problems. So a team needs people with different personalities and different characteristics to combine.
