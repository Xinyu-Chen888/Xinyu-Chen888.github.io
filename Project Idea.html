Overview

This is an online shopping tourism software based on Google map. When it is not convenient for people to go out, such as a rainstorm, sun exposure, special period (COVID-19), people can use this app to simulate travel, travel, and make friends and chat. The app uses a 2D map and panoramic map technology, and the user controls a villain to move in the application. And it's moving at real speed. Chat with people on the same screen and even chat in the same area.

Description

The biggest selling point of this software is an online travel and real friends simulation. The first is the online travel function. Each user builds and controls a simulation character of his own. The high degree of freedom character design allows users to create very personalized and interesting characters by pinching their faces, including various kinds of robots, monsters, cute animals and other rich choices. Users can control the movement of personages with rich personalities in the map, and the mobile mode within the application is carried out in the virtual model, such as walking, cycling, and aeroplane. All of these vehicles run at pseudo speed. When users move in the software at real speed and move in the street, they can observe the surroundings with a panoramic view (Google map street view), so as to achieve the feeling of virtual tourism. Players can use the third-person perspective of the 2D map to play, or the first person called the street view to play. At the same time, the map combines the real road and city characteristics, when users enter different areas, there will be different environmental effects triggered, simulate different climate, weather, including rainstorm, drought, desert, snow. Users can punch in the application to travel around the world, including the pyramid, the Arc de Triomphe, the great wall and other famous scenic spots. There will be historical stories and records of each scenic spot in the app, which will take users to understand why each scenic spot has become a classic. Provide ar play, you can synthesize your own photos into the scene, and take some interesting photos. And linked with Google map, you can view the local real information, store links, and simulate the real shopping scene. This means that the links you click in the application are all from the real information of Google map. You can shop in the online stores of merchants, and even buy special souvenirs from different places. This means that even though you are not out of the house, you can still shop like a real trip.

At the same time, the software also provides open social function, players in the same area can see each other, and simulate the outdoor reality, users without adding friends can only chat with each other when the game characters are close Users can initiate private chat with users around the role and add them as friends, or join public voice to chat with a range of partners. Even users can choose to play alone, refuse other people's information, and enjoy the pleasure of travelling alone.

Users can invite other users to chat on the screen to simulate the scene of face-to-face chat. This kind of virtual outdoor friend making method will make users look brand new.
